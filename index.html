Introduction

From childhood, I was fascinated by technology. It was the era of 2G when I got my first computer. Since then, I used to be curious about how the User Interface works. I was also very intrigued by computer hardware, like the Processor, Motherboard, RAM, GPU, etc.

Then, gradually when I was in my 11th standard, I knew what's best for me, so I opted for Science with Computer Science as my additional subject. Till 12th standard, I used to make small applications using C++, which used to be black screened. Afterward, I went on for my Bachelor's degree in Information Technology. There I came to know about many internal workings of a computer, making advanced applications, solving problems, etc. Till now, I have the same hunger for knowledge, but the purpose is different.

I also possess hobbies, like Photography, Cooking, and Coding. I got my first DSLR as a gift from my brother, and I capture plenty of pictures using it. You can have a look at some of them in the Images section.
